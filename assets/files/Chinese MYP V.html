COURSE Objectives 教學目標﹕

After one year of learning, the students will be able to:

         Recognize 133 Chinese characters in the book

         Make phrases and make sentences with phrases

         Be able to write a short composition

         Fluent with 15-20 everyday dialogues

 

INSTRUCTIONAL MATERIALS 教學教材

Basic Text 主要教材:

  • Chinese Character Flash cards
  • Character recognition contest
