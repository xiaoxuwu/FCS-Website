"The Folsom Chinese School welcomes students of all backgrounds to embark on the fruitful journey of learning and reinforcing Chinese, a language of growing importance globally and here in the US."

Students will:

  • Learn Chinese from dedicated and skilled Chinese Teachers

  • Be surrounded by other friendly students in a Chinese Environment

  • Take part in fun Chinese activities, events, and celebrations

  • Be exposed to scholarship opportunities

To enroll, please download and fill out the registration form above. You may either come to the school with the form and fee or submit them to our mailing address. Please make the check payable to: Folsom Chinese School. For more information, check out Classes and About.

We ensure that all rights, privileges, programs, and activities are accorded equally to students at our school. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, nationality or ethnic origin in the administration of our educational policies, admissions policies and other school-administered programs.
